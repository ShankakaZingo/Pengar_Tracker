Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects information that you voluntarily provide while using the Service, including expense entries, categories, descriptions, dates, and amounts. We may also collect basic account information such as your email address and username for authentication purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the expense tracking functionality of Spendly. This includes displaying your spending summaries, generating category breakdowns, and enabling filtering by time periods. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely in a local SQLite database. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no system is completely secure, and you use the Service at your own risk. You are responsible for backing up any important data you enter.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services such as Google Fonts for typography. These services may collect limited information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not share your financial or personal data with any third-party services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the application.
